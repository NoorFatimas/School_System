Subjects Offered

At GreenLeaf International School, we offer a wide range of subjects to build strong academic foundations and nurture creativity.

Subject Grades Description
English Language Grade 1 – Grade 10 Develops reading, writing, and communication skills with a focus on grammar and creative expression.
Mathematics Grade 1 – Grade 10 Encourages problem-solving and logical reasoning through practical exercises and fun activities.
Science Grade 3 – Grade 10 Explores biology, chemistry, and physics concepts, promoting curiosity and critical thinking through experiments.
Computer Studies Grade 4 – Grade 10 Introduces students to basic computing, programming, and internet safety, preparing them for the digital world.
Social Studies Grade 3 – Grade 10 Teaches history, geography, and civics to help students understand the world and their role as responsible citizens.
Urdu Grade 1 – Grade 10 Strengthens reading, writing, and cultural appreciation of the national language through stories and poetry.
Islamic Studies Grade 1 – Grade 10 Instills moral values and religious teachings, encouraging students to practice honesty, kindness, and respect.
Arts & Crafts Grade 1 – Grade 8 Encourages imagination and creativity through drawing, painting, and other artistic activities.
